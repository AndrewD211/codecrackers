How the Caesar Cipher Works

The Caesar cipher is what's called a substitution cipher. Each letter is replaced by another letter a fixed number of places down the alphabet.

HELLO+3KHOOR

Pretty straightforward, right? You'll see why it works (and how to break it) in a second.
